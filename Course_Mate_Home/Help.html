Navigation

To access different pages on our website, use the navigation bar at the top and click on the buttons. Click on the CourseMate Logo to go back to the site's main page. Click on the Search button to look for a document. Click on the Upload button to upload documents to our database. Click on the Courses button to access our available documents for each course. If you are trying to log out, or need help, mouse-click the Other button and a drop down menu will appear. Select the option you are looking for.
